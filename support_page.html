Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan food with the camera?
Tap the camera button on the home screen, point at your food, and the AI will automatically identify and calculate nutrition information.
How do I change my calorie goal?
Go to Profile ‚Üí Nutrition Goals ‚Üí Edit your calorie goal. The app will automatically adjust your macro targets.
Can I export my data?
Yes! Premium users can export their data to PDF from Profile ‚Üí Export Data.
How do I create a diet plan?
Go to the Diet tab (or History if you have an active plan), tap "Create Diet Plan", and follow the setup wizard.
